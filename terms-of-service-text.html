Terms of service

The Automatic Weather Station (AWS) data available here is level 3 processed (L3) and updated hourly with near-real-time transmitted data from the Greenland ice sheet. The data is considered provisional. If you will be using the data in publication (or for a public-facing service or product), please cite the relevant DOI citation listed at the GEUS Dataverse. AWS data at the GEUS Dataverse is a curated archive that is updated monthly.

AWS data is available in both NetCDF and CSV formats, in two directory structures:

  • "aws_l3_station" contains files in station-specific sub-directories.
  • "aws_l3_time" contains files for all stations in hourly, daily, and monthly sub-directories.

"level_3" directories contain field-retrieved data from station loggers, joined with transmitted data for the period since the last station visit. "tx" directories contain only L3 transmitted data, including instantaneous samples for air pressure, temperature, humidity, wind speed and direction.
